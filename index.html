My Story

About Me

Tyrant TCG Limited was founded in 2024, born out of a lifelong passion for trading card games — from collecting Pokémon and playing Yu-Gi-Oh! growing up to turning that love into a business. Based in Colorado and run personally by one dedicated seller, every order is handled with care from start to finish.

I currently operate exclusively online through TCGplayer, with in-person events on the horizon. Yu-Gi-Oh! and Pokémon are my strongest areas of expertise, but I carry and know my way around all four games in my store. Orders typically ship within 1 business day.

For private business inquiries, please reach out directly via email.
